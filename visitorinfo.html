Food and Drink Policy

Food and drinks that are not bought from inside the zoo are not allowed. However, you are allowed to bring items such as bottled water and some small snacks.

Pet Policy

Unfortunately, because of state and federal law, you are not allowed to bring any pets in the zoo. The only pets you can bring are service animals which you will have to sign in.

Lost and Found

The lost and found is held next to the park's entrance and is called the Guest Experience Office.
